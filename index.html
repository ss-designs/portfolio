Services

Here's My Process:

  • Brainstorming & Conceptual Thinking
  • This is where we go over your hopes, fears and dreams of what you want your website to look like.
  • Sketching & Research Analysis
  • Here we'll make some rough sketches and start listing everything together that you want on your website.
  • Wireframing - lo to hi end wireframe design
  • Now we'll go through and create a layout to what your website will look like & how it'll be used.
  • Coding Wireframe
  • From ideas to reality! Here we code your web design to make it into a fully functioning website!
  • Responsive Design
  • After coding the website, we'll make sure it looks fantastic on all devices!
  • Testing & Reviewing
  • Second to our last step, we take a good overlook of what we've created and make sure that everything is working out correctly.
  • Final Setup and Launching your Websites
  • Houston, We're going Live!
